📍 About CrowdMapper

CrowdMapper is an innovative civic technology platform that harnesses the power of crowdsourcing to address local community issues. By bridging the gap between citizens and municipal authorities, we create a more responsive and accountable urban environment.

The platform enables citizens to report problems, verify reports from others, and track the resolution of issues in real-time. Our mission is to empower every resident to become an active participant in their city's development.

🎯 Our Objectives

  • Democratize civic issue reporting and monitoring
  • Ensure credibility through community-driven verification
  • Optimize resource allocation for municipal authorities
  • Promote transparency in public service delivery
  • Enable data-driven urban planning and policy making

⚙️ How It Works

Our platform follows a transparent 4-step process to ensure every reported issue gets the attention it deserves.

📢 STEP 1: Citizen Reports Problem

Citizens use the platform to report local issues like potholes, garbage dumps, or water leakages by providing location details and descriptions.

👥 STEP 2: Community Verification

Other residents in the same area can verify the report, adding strength to the complaint and ensuring only genuine issues are escalated.

🤖 STEP 3: Smart Prioritization

The system calculates an urgency score based on the number of verifications and the severity of the issue reported.

✅ STEP 4: Authority Action & Feedback

Authorities access the admin dashboard to approve, assign, and resolve issues, with real-time updates provided back to the citizens.
